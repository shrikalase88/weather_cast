# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: June 2026

## Overview

Weather Cast is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **GPS Coordinates**: We collect your device's GPS coordinates only when you explicitly enable Location Services for the app
- **Purpose**: To fetch accurate weather data for your current location
- **Storage**: Location data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
- **User Control**: You can disable location sharing at any time through device settings

### Weather Information
- We request weather data from OpenWeatherMap API based on the location coordinates you provide
- This information is not stored on our servers
- Data is cached locally on your device for improved performance

### User Preferences
- Saved locations are stored locally on your device using secure local storage
- PIN settings are encrypted and stored locally
- This data never leaves your device

### Automatic Information
- We do not collect analytics data
- We do not use tracking pixels or similar technologies
- We do not collect device identifiers or IDFA

## Data Processing

All data processing occurs:
- **Locally on your device**: Location storage, preferences, cache
- **With OpenWeatherMap**: Real-time weather data requests only contain location coordinates

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT** share any personal data with:
- Third-party advertisers
- Analytics services
- Marketing platforms
- Data brokers

The only external service we communicate with is OpenWeatherMap, which receives:
- Your location coordinates (latitude/longitude)
- Your IP address (standard HTTP header)

Weather Cast does not receive, process, or store data from these requests.

## Security

We implement multiple security measures:
- API keys are not stored in the application binary
- All API communications use HTTPS
- Local data is encrypted using platform-provided security
- No plain-text passwords or sensitive credentials

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access what data we collect about you
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of location sharing
- Delete saved locations at any time

Since all data is stored locally, you have complete control and can delete everything by:
- **iOS**: Settings > General > iPhone Storage > Weather Cast > Offload or Delete App
- **Android**: Settings > Apps > Weather Cast > Uninstall

## Data Retention

We retain no data on our servers. All data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

## Children's Privacy

Weather Cast does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the application following notification of changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## Contact Us

For privacy concerns or questions:
- Review your device settings where all stored data is managed locally
- Uninstall and reinstall the app to clear all data
